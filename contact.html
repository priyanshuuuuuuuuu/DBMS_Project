Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our service and platform.

How does FoodLoop help reduce food waste?

FoodLoop connects food providers with excess food to companies that can process it into valuable resources. By creating this circular economy, we help divert food waste from landfills and turn it into useful products like compost, animal feed, or biogas.

How can my restaurant or food business join FoodLoop?

Joining is simple! Click on the "Sign Up" button at the top of the page and select "Food Provider" as your role. Fill in your business details, and our team will guide you through the onboarding process. You'll be able to start listing your surplus food in no time.

What types of food waste can be processed through your platform?

Our platform handles various types of food waste, including vegetable scraps, fruit peels, coffee grounds, used cooking oil, expired dairy products, stale bread, and more. Different processor companies on our platform specialize in handling specific types of food waste.

How are transportation logistics handled?

FoodLoop has a network of transportation partners who specialize in food waste collection. Once a match is made between a provider and processor, our system automatically schedules a pickup with the nearest available transporter, optimizing routes for efficiency and minimal environmental impact.
